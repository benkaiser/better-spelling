Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 20, 2026 · Developer: Benjamin Kaiser · Contact: adultspelling@benkaiser.dev

Overview

Better Spelling for Adults is a spelling practice app available on iOS, Android, macOS, and the web. Your privacy matters, and this app is designed to keep your data under your control. Here's exactly what the app does and doesn't do with your information.

Data Stored on Your Device

All of your progress and preferences — including known words, mastered words, your practice bank, and settings — are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences (or equivalent local storage on web). This data is never uploaded to any server.

If you uninstall the app or clear its data, this information will be lost.

Data Sent to Our Server

When you add custom words, the app sends those words to our server to generate context sentences for your practice sessions.

  • What is sent: Only the words you submit.
  • What is NOT sent: No personal information, no device identifiers, no usage data — just the words themselves.

This is the only network communication the app initiates (aside from in-app purchases, described below).

Text-to-Speech

The app uses your device's built-in text-to-speech engine to read words aloud. No audio is recorded, stored, or transmitted.

In-App Purchases

The app offers a single non-consumable in-app purchase for premium features. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play Store). We do not receive, process, or store any payment or billing information.

For details on how Apple or Google handle your payment data, please refer to their respective privacy policies:

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

What We Don't Do

  • No user accounts or login — there is nothing to sign up for.
  • No analytics or tracking — we don't collect usage statistics.
  • No advertising — there are no ads in the app.
  • No cookies — the web version does not use cookies.
  • No data sharing with third parties — apart from the word list sent for sentence generation and payment processing handled by Apple/Google, no data leaves your device.

Children's Privacy

This app is designed for adults and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted here with an updated effective date. Since we don't collect contact information, we have no way to notify you directly — so please check back occasionally.
