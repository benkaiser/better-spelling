Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Overview

Better Spelling is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe your spelling practice is your own business.

Data We Collect

Nothing leaves your device. All progress data, statistics, and preferences are stored locally on your device using on-device storage. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

Text-to-Speech

The app uses your device's built-in text-to-speech engine to read words aloud. No audio data is sent to external services.

Children's Privacy

This app is designed for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes

If we ever update this policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated date.
